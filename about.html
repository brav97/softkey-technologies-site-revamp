About us

Our vision

SOFTKEY TECHNOLOGIES is a private company which was incorporated in 2017. We are specialists in the field of Stainless-Steel Fabrications, Reverse Osmosis Machines, Mixing Tanks, Cooling tanks, Milk Batch pasteurizers, industrial automation with Unitronics Programmable Logic Controllers. Our machines are custom made to meet the requirements and needs of our dear clients. We are the industry leaders in water purification and treatment. We have invested in Ultra-Modern Water purifiers with the sole aim of fulfilling your personal and corporate water solutions. At SOFTKEY TECHNOLOGIES we also deal in stainless steel fabrication. we deal with all grades from 201 to 316 according to the standards and requirements. From 1.0MM TO 6MM in thickness. We have experts in welding SS joints in water piping with Zero leaks. All our water treatment plants from pure water to salty water are always made as per the lab tests done on raw water. All the filtrations from pretreatment to final treatment is done in regard to professional testing. Our mixing tanks are made from 500 LTRS to any big volume as required. We serve several industries; Notably Medical and Dairy Industries. We have worked on diverse projects, ranging from the installation of small-scale water filling station equipment to larger-scale water purification solutions in real estate projects (Shelter Villa, Ruai; Pikadili resort in Kendu Bay, and Better Life springs, Thika)
